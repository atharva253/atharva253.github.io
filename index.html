Atharva Anand Joshi

Hi everyone! I am Atharva Anand Joshi, a final year undergraduate student at Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani majoring in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. During my undergrad, I have worked on research projects under the guidance of Dr Syed Mohammad Zafaruddin (SM Research Group) and Dr Ananthakrishna Chintanpalli. My research interests include deep learning, representation learning, signal processing, computer vision, and artificial intelligence.

Presently, I am working as an intern in the AI Deployment Core Team of American Express, AI Labs . Our goal is to make the develop-to-deploy journey quick, seamless and efficient. I am developing a framework to facilitate an end-to-end Self Learning Pipeline for Sequence Models. This would considerably reduce the time and manual effort required to get a model up and running to solve business problems.

In the past, I’ve had the opportunity to intern at Adobe Research, Bangalore with mentors Dr Atanu Sinha and Dr Sunav Choudhary. Here, we created concise user representations which can be projected onto edge server, providing faster marketing services. I have also worked on explainable computer vision / deep learning for medical imaging applications at CSIR – Central Electronics Engineering Research Institute.

Feel free to reach out for research collaboration, academic guidance (if you are an undergraduate student from BITS) or even just for a chat! For GRE and TOEFL preparation guidance, especially under time contraints do check out this repository.
